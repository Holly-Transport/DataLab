Who does what? What do I need to learn? The DEC Data Group is demystifying data career streams, starting with a new Skill Badge system.

The Development Economics Data Group is tasked with working with HR to establish the data professional career stream. While this may take some time, in the interim, to meet the urgent need to identify relevant data skills for forming multi-disciplinary teams, the Lab proposes establishing a certification, or “badge” system. The Lab will establish criteria for staff to apply for badges they can affix to their email signatures – Data Science Level 1-3; Geographer Level 1-3; Data Visualization Level 1-3; etc. -- with descriptions of the skills associated with each of these available through link to the Lab website. The Lab would then maintain a repository of staff with these skillsets, able to support formation of multi-disciplinary teams and staff the rotating Data Corps. ​ Critically, the training and workshop program will underpin the badge system, giving staff the ability to laser-focus their learning on those activities that will help them “level up” in the badge system. ​ ​ Want to join the Skill Badge team?
